# 外贸客户开发话术模板

## 一、邮件开发基础

### 1.1 开发信结构（公式AIDA）

```
A - Attention（引起注意）
  └── 开头一句话吸引注意力

I - Interest（激发兴趣）
  └── 展示你能提供的价值

D - Desire（激发欲望）
  └── 具体说明客户能得到什么

A - Action（行动号召）
  └── 明确下一步动作
```

### 1.2 开发信黄金法则

| 原则 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| 简短 | 首次邮件不超过100词 |
| 个性化 | 提及客户公司/产品 |
| 价值导向 | 强调能为客户带来什么 |
| 明确CTA | 告诉客户下一步 |
| 多版本测试 | A/B测试优化 |

## 二、首次开发信模板

### 模板1：产品切入型

**标题**：[对方公司名] - Could [产品] Save You 15% on Costs?

**正文**：

```
Hi [姓名],

I noticed [对方公司名] is [提到对方的业务/产品].

We supply [产品] to [行业] companies in [对方国家/地区], helping them reduce costs by 10-20%.

Would you be open to a brief call this week to explore if this could benefit your business?

Best regards,
[你的名字]
[职位]
[公司名]
[联系方式]
```

### 模板2：问题切入型

**标题**：Quick question about [对方公司名]'s [痛点问题]

**正文**：

```
Hi [姓名],

I'm reaching out because many [行业] companies face [痛点问题] when sourcing from China.

We've helped [X] companies solve this, including [一个知名客户/案例].

Could we schedule a 15-minute call this week to discuss how we might help?

Thanks,
[你的名字]
```

### 模板3：熟人推荐型

**标题**：[共同联系人的名字] suggested I reach out

**正文**：

```
Hi [姓名],

[共同联系人名字] mentioned your company might be looking for a reliable [产品] supplier.

We specialize in [产品类别] and work with [目标市场] companies. [简短说明优势]

Would you be available for a quick call this week?

Best,
[你的名字]
```

### 模板4：展示型（适合有明确产品的工厂）

**标题**：[产品名] Manufacturer - [核心卖点]

**正文**：

```
Hi [姓名],

We are a professional [产品名] manufacturer in China, specializing in [产品特点/应用领域].

What makes us different:
- [优势1，如：15+ years OEM experience]
- [优势2，如：ISO 9001/CE/FCC certified]
- [优势3，如：Monthly capacity 50,000 pcs]
- [优势4，如：Sample in 5 days, MOQ 100 pcs]

I'd love to learn about your requirements. Can we connect this week?

Regards,
[你的名字]
[职位]
[公司名]
[WhatsApp/WeChat]
```

## 三、Follow-up邮件序列

### 3.1 标准跟进序列（5封）

**第1封**：首次开发信（Day 1）
**第2封**：跟进邮件（Day 4）
**第3封**：价值补充（Day 8）
**第4封**：最后机会（Day 15）
**第5封**：温和维护（Day 30）

### 3.2 跟进邮件内容

**第2封 - 跟进（Day 4）**

```
Subject: Re: [第1封标题] - Quick follow up

Hi [姓名],

Just following up on my previous email about [主题].

Did you get a chance to review it? Happy to answer any questions.

If now is not a good time, just let me know. Otherwise, could we schedule a quick call?

Thanks,
[你的名字]
```

**第3封 - 价值补充（Day 8）**

```
Subject: [产品] Update - May interest you

Hi [姓名],

I wanted to share a quick update:

[分享一个有价值的信息，如：新产品/行业趋势/优惠活动]

This might be relevant to [对方公司] given your focus on [对方业务].

Would you like to learn more?

Best,
[你的名字]
```

**第4封 - 最后机会（Day 15）**

```
Subject: One last try - [产品] for [对方行业]

Hi [姓名],

I understand you are busy. I'll close my file for now, but wanted to leave you with this:

[总结你能提供的核心价值，1-2句话]

If you ever need [产品] in the future, feel free to reach out.

Best wishes,
[你的名字]
```

**第5封 - 温和维护（Day 30）**

```
Subject: Just checking in

Hi [姓名],

Haven't heard from you in a while. Just checking in.

We [做了什么更新，如：launched new products / updated our catalog].

If you'd like, I'd be happy to send you our latest catalog.

No pressure - just here if you need us.

Best,
[你的名字]
```

## 四、LinkedIn开发话术

### 4.1 LinkedIn连接请求消息

**模板1：简洁版**
```
Hi [姓名], I help [目标客户] companies source quality [产品] from China. Would love to connect.
```

**模板2：个性化版**
```
Hi [姓名], Noticed you work at [公司名] and handle [采购/供应链相关职责]. I specialize in helping [行业] companies find reliable [产品] suppliers. Let's connect!
```

**模板3：行业切入版**
```
Hi [姓名], As a [行业] professional, I thought you might find our [产品] solutions relevant. Would you be open to a quick connection?
```

### 4.2 LinkedIn InMail模板

**发送给采购经理**：

```
Hi [姓名],

I noticed [公司名] is [提及对方的业务或动态].

We supply [产品] to [行业] companies and help them:
- [痛点1的解决方案]
- [痛点2的解决方案]
- [核心优势]

Would you be open to a 15-minute call to discuss your current [采购需求/痛点]?

Best,
[你的名字]
[公司名]
```

### 4.3 LinkedIn post评论话术

当你在对方帖子下评论时：

**真诚互动型**：
```
Great insights on [帖子主题]! We've seen similar trends with our [行业] clients in [市场]. One thing we found particularly interesting was [提及具体观点]. Keep sharing!
```

**价值补充型**：
```
Solid points! At [你的公司], we work with [目标客户] on this exact challenge. Happy to share our perspective if helpful.
```

## 五、针对不同客户类型的应对

### 5.1 大买家（采购量大）

**识别特征**：公司规模大，采购流程规范

**话术重点**：
- 强调质量认证和产能
- 提供案例和客户参考
- 表示愿意通过对方验厂

**示例**：
```
We understand [大买家公司] has high standards for quality and compliance. We hold [认证列表] and have supplied similar companies like [参考客户]. We'd welcome your audit at any time.
```

### 5.2 中间商/贸易商

**识别特征**：中间商，代理采购

**话术重点**：
- 讨论合作模式
- 强调保密性
- 说明价格竞争力

**示例**：
```
As a trading company, you need a supplier who respects your client confidentiality. We have extensive experience working with [贸易商/中间商] and can offer competitive pricing while protecting your client information.
```

### 5.3 电商卖家

**识别特征**：平台店铺，需要快速发货

**话术重点**：
- 强调交期和灵活性
- 提供样品支持
- 说明包装定制能力

**示例**：
```
We work with Amazon and eBay sellers, offering:
- Low MOQ for testing (50-100 pcs)
- FBA-ready packaging
- 7-day sample production
- 15-day mass production
Happy to send samples for your review.
```

### 5.4 工程商/项目采购

**识别特征**：项目制采购，需要方案支持

**话术重点**：
- 技术支持能力
- 定制开发能力
- 项目经验

**示例**：
```
For [项目类型] projects, we can provide:
- Technical specifications matching your requirements
- Customized solutions for [应用场景]
- References from similar projects
Our team includes [X] engineers ready to support your project.
```

## 六、常见拒绝话术破解

### 6.1 "We already have suppliers"

```
Thanks for letting me know. Many of our current clients said the same before working with us.

Would you mind if I sent you a brief overview? No commitment needed - just for your reference in case your current supplier doesn't work out.
```

### 6.2 "Not interested right now"

```
Completely understand. Would it be helpful if I check in again in [时间，如：3 months]?

In the meantime, here's our latest catalog: [链接] - feel free to review when you have time.
```

### 6.3 "Your price is too high"

```
I appreciate your feedback on pricing. Could you share what you're currently paying?

I want to make sure we're comparing apples to apples - sometimes there are significant quality and service differences that affect the true cost.
```

### 6.4 "Send me your catalog"

```
Happy to! To make sure I send you the most relevant products, could you tell me:
- What [产品类别] are you primarily looking for?
- Any specific applications or requirements?

This helps me narrow down to the best options for you.
```
